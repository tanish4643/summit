    Automobile Injury Relief

    If you are a victim of an automobile accident, let Summit Chiropractic help you find relief from your injuries. Common injuries resulting from motor vehicle accidents include damage to the soft tissues of the spinal muscles and ligaments, and this is a treatment area where chiropractors excel. 

    Visit our site in Champlin or Maplewood when you need chiropractic care.

    Seek Treatment as Soon as Possible

    Your injuries may not be obvious right away. It can take weeks or months for symptoms to arise. Be sure to seek treatment as soon as possible to determine whether you’ve been injured and to substantiate any claim to monetary compensation for medical bills, lost wages, and other settlements from the driver who caused the incident.

    Professional Decisions About Your Condition

    Our staff will determine:
    • The extent of the injury or injuries
    • The number of estimated treatments required 
    • If a referral to another medical specialist is required
    • If special tests are required (i.e., MRI, CT scan, etc.)
    • If you need to take injury time off from work (providing you with a note to your employer)
